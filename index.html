Space Quad

Problems look mighty small from 150 miles up

Space Quad is a innovative solution for dammage dectection on ISS, with advanced technologye and well thinked ideas, VAMUdeS Space made possible to produce a solution able to solve problems on ISS.

The Platform

Main technical features

  • Canadarm docking capabilities
  • Main structure made of Carbon Fiber that could be coated to protect from radiation
  • 2 modules of 4 RCS Thrusters for complete range of motion
  • 2 360 degrees lidar sensor to detect and avoir colisions
  • 8 solar panels
  • RCS gas storage with refueling capability

Blind spot accessibility

Able to identify damage currently not accessible from windows or Canadarm

Compatible with Canadarm

Can navigate around ISS with Canadarm.

Lithium Ion batteries

  • Performance Advantages
    • Lightweight
    • Low self discharge rates
  • Reduced System Complexity
    • High cell operating voltage (3.5 volts)
  • Conformable geometry - cells are flexible
    • Solid State Construction
    • No free electrolyte
    • Reduced cost
  • Smart batteries
    • Independent cell charge/discharge controllers
    • Keeps cells in balance
    • Extends cycle life
    • Reduced heat generation rates

Solar panels

Enables self battery charge. With low power consumption in idle mode, it is possible to charge batteries efficiently without any other source of energy than solar power.

3D Camera Sensor

Stereoscopic camera for 3D imagery make it possible to get crystal clear images and analyse more efficiently data received. It can also analyse more precisly the location of anomalies with overlap between multiple pictures. Easy analysis with imagery software. UV filter for better clarity images and shadow filtering. Image comparison analysis based on location for better data processing results

  • First line sensor for the inspection
  • Stereoscopic camera for 3D imagery
  • High precision location of anomalies
  • Easy analysis with imagery software
  • UV filter for better clarity images and shadow filtering
  • Image comparison analysis based on location for better data processing results

LiDAR sensor

  • Holes detection
  • Multiple view angles for maximum precision
  • Damage depth analysis
  • Region of interest classification of based on damage gravity

Automatic data analysis

  • Detects differences between previous scans and identifies new damage
  • Uses correlation to automatically detect problem areas
  • Problem areas are transmitted to ground control or to crew for further analysis

Laser localisation

  • Distance measurement based on time-of-flight of a laser beam reflected on strategically disposed retroreflectors
  • Space Quad is located using triangulation
  • Multiple laser sensor for redundancy and stable connection
  • Location calibration based on the center of ISS
  • Calibration at every mission to ensure maximum precision

Autonomous missions

The Space Quad handles custom mission planning for more effecient missions when needed. This means the mission times will vary depending on the type of mission that is made. If a full automatic mission is made, data acquiring can take up to an hour, but if a specific region of the ISS is targeted, a mission could take as low as fifteen minutes. Live feedback status of the drone is shown on the mission software, this ensures eveything is going as scheduled during the mission. It is possible to have manua takeouver is necessary. Three autonomous controller are installed for redundancy and high reliability.

Data Transmission

Live data transmission to ISS. Data processing on ISS. Reduces payload size. Enables more control over data processing. Easier software updates

Safe distance flight

15-20 m from ISS. Close enough for detailed images. Safe distance for manual takeover. Reduced mission time with more data per images
